About Us

Our Story

The Confederation of Valuers (COV) is a Section 8 not-for-profit organisation committed to elevating the valuation profession in India and contributing to its global advancement. As a trusted professional body, we provide a structured ecosystem for valuers at all stages—students, early-career professionals, and seasoned experts—offering pathways for skill development, leadership, and specialised expertise.

We actively collaborate with individuals, academic institutions, valuation firms, and industry stakeholders to promote ethical practice, continuous learning, and global standards. Whether you're entering the field or shaping its future, COV provides the platform to connect, contribute, and lead with integrity.

Our Mission

Empowering the Future of Valuation

To cultivate a thriving ecosystem where valuation professionals excel through continuous learning, ethical practice, and innovative collaboration—driving sustainable economic impact and setting new benchmarks for the industry worldwide.
